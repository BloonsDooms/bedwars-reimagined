# Bedwars Reimagined
...something different<sup>TM</sup>

## Core Design
Two teams, control points rather than centeral generators. Streamlined shops/inventory management.



## Ideas

### Swappable Tools
Hold right click to swap to the correct tool, thus only using one hotbar slot (rather than three)

### Reloading wool/arrows
Wool (and arrows if you have a bow) reloads automatically, and has a cap. For wool, both of those can be upgraded for the team. For bows, that is tied to the level of the bow.
For wool, this is intended to let you always have _some_ blocks, but not an instant, infinite supply. For arrows, this is primarially to limit blatant bow spam.

### Melee Combat
Swords are given about 30% faster attacks (pacing reasons), and lower damage to compensate. They can right click to block 80% of a projectile's damage, and 50% of melee damage.
All melee weapons disable the ability to block attacks breifly, at 100% chance.

### Currency Items
Not actual items, they are displayed above or to the side of your hotbar. While in the shop, it will display next to the shop.
Currently leaning towards three items (iron gold diamond), but only if I can make them feel distinct.
